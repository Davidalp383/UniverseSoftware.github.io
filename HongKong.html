University of Hong Kong (HKU)

HKU is the oldest institution of higher education in Hong Kong. Its motto is "Sapientia et virtus" in Latin, which translates to "Wisdom and Virtue." The official language of instruction is English.

According to Quacquarelli Symonds, HKU is a "world-class comprehensive research university" and was ranked 24th in the 2009 THES - QS World University Rankings, making it the 2nd in Asia (after the University of Tokyo). In 2009, it was ranked 1st among Asian universities by Quacquarelli Symonds. HKU was also included in Newsweek's 2006 ranking of the top 100 global universities.

This university offers a wide range of career-focused programs in the development of new technologies, sciences, and systems. In total, it provides 12 programs.
