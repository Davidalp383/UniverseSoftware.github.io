University of California, Berkeley

Another of the top technological universities in the world is the University of Berkeley. It stands out, especially for having high-level engineering programs with worldwide renown. The tuition cost is quite high, so most students either have a high socioeconomic status or have access to scholarships. In fact, many classes have fewer than twenty students, making the learning experience truly exclusive.

For this reason, many students who undergo their education at this university end up becoming entrepreneurs in the world of technology. They become capable of identifying market needs and developing technological gadgets that have become essential in our daily lives.
