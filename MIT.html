Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

MIT, which stands for Massachusetts Institute of Technology in English, is a private university located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, United States. It is widely recognized and considered by numerous rankings as one of the best and most prestigious universities worldwide, holding the title of the world's best university for ten consecutive years according to the QS World University Rankings.

In this university, there is no defined curriculum; students can choose the subjects they want to take each semester. The university focuses on developing technology-oriented skills in students, so each class is geared towards software development, hardware, mechatronics, or robotics.
