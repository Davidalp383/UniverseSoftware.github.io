Cairo University

It is a higher education institute located in Giza, Egypt. Cairo University is home to a Faculty of Law and a Faculty of Medicine. The latter was one of the first medical faculties in Africa and the Middle East, with its initial building being a donation from Alaini Pasha.

This university has one of the best funding allocations focused on technologies and computer systems. The emphasis is placed on various engineering disciplines offered by the university, ranging from computer engineering/systems/electrical engineering to civil engineering. The university channels its most modern technological resources into the education of students pursuing these fields. As a result, it is now recognized as the institution that produces the highest quality of graduates in these areas in Egypt and a significant portion of East Africa.

It is worth mentioning that the university also emphasizes the use of modern technologies in other fields it offers, such as medicine, dentistry, physics, and astronomy.
