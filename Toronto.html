University of Toronto

The University of Toronto, located in Toronto, Ontario, is the largest university in Canada. It attracts students from across Canada and northern New York, as well as a significant number of students from other countries. The university has produced five Canadian prime ministers, three governors-general, and numerous internationally renowned business and academic leaders. It boasts the highest number of Nobel Prize winners among Canadian universities.

Although not frequently mentioned or well-known, this university offers high-quality education across all its fields and programs. In fact, it leads in the development of AI programs worldwide, and its technological programs compete in educational quality with the most famous universities in the United States. Moreover, it is one of the most accessible universities for international students, providing lucrative scholarships and student benefits to address the country's workforce demands.
