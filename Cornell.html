Cornell University

It is an academic institution of higher education belonging to the prestigious Ivy League. Located in Ithaca, New York, it has two additional campuses, one in New York City and another in Doha, Qatar. Cornell University is considered one of the top 10 research universities in the United States and globally, according to the Academic Ranking of World Universities. Its student population is 21,904, making it the Ivy League university with the highest number of students.

According to QS 2022, Cornell University ranks among the top 10 universities globally in subjects such as Computer Science and Cybersecurity. Additionally, it ranks among the top 20 globally in subjects like Biological Sciences, Engineering, and Business Administration.

This university offers an efficient and sophisticated study methodology. In 2021, research on this study methodology was initiated, and it has now become a publicly available study method, applicable for any student researching it online.
