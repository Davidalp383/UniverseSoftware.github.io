École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)

The École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) is a university in Lausanne, Switzerland. Located in the heart of Europe, it is one of the leading European institutions in science and technology.

The school was founded by the Swiss Federal Council with the following objectives:

Train engineers and scientists. Be a national center of excellence in science and technology. Serve as a point of interaction between the scientific community and industry.

This university has the broadest support from scientific institutions globally, with over 71 institutions and independent entities focusing on various areas of science and technology working in collaboration with it. Among these institutions, more than 24 are entirely dedicated to computer systems, software development, cybersecurity, digitization, and information encryption, among many others.
