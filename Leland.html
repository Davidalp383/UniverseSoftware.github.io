Leland Stanford Junior University

Stanford University, known as Leland Stanford Junior University in English, is a private American university located in Stanford, California, about 56 km southeast of San Francisco. Stanford is renowned for the quality of its teaching, its wealth, and its proximity to Silicon Valley, the birthplace of some of the most important high-tech companies. It is considered one of the top ten universities in the world.

This university is famous for having the best programs in the fields of computer engineering, electrical engineering, and business sciences, ranking in the top 10 of the best universities in the U.S. in these areas.

Professionals graduating from this university focus on the development and maintenance of database systems for multinational private or government enterprises. This indicates an institution-wide focus on offering programs that foster the capabilities of data analysts and cybersecurity experts.

Other professionals graduating from the electrical engineering program are trained to create software systems for the maintenance of power plants, as well as for the development and design of power generation systems for public companies providing this service.
